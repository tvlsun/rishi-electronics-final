About Our Shop

Visit us at our convenient location for all your electronics repair needs. We've been serving the community with dedication and expertise.

Our Repair Services

We provide expert repair services for a wide range of electronic devices and home appliances. Quality service guaranteed!

TV Repair

All brands of LED, LCD, Smart TVs. Expert diagnosis and repair of display issues, power problems, and software updates.

Laptop Repair

Hardware and software repairs, screen replacement, battery replacement, keyboard repair, and system upgrades.

Mobile Repair

Screen replacement, battery change, water damage repair, charging port repair, and software troubleshooting.

Home Theater

Installation, repair, and maintenance of home theater systems. Audio/video troubleshooting and upgrades.

Inverter

Inverter and UPS repair, battery replacement, circuit board repair, and backup power solutions.

Washing Machine

Repair of all types - front load, top load, semi-automatic. Motor repair, drum replacement, and maintenance.

Refrigerator

Cooling issues, compressor repair, gas filling, thermostat replacement, and regular maintenance services.

Air Conditioner

AC installation, repair, gas charging, cleaning, and annual maintenance contracts for all AC types.
